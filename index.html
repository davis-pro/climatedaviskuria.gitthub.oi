What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term changes in temperature, precipitation, and other atmospheric conditions. Human activity, particularly the burning of fossil fuels, has accelerated climate change at an unprecedented rate.

The Impact of Climate Change

Climate change is leading to rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and melting ice caps.
